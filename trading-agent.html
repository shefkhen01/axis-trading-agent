AXIS

Bitget AI Base Camp Hackathon S1 · Track 1

BTC Adaptive Trend + Mean-Reversion Agent

AXIS autonomously perceives the BTC market, decides a direction, executes simulated trades, and manages risk on its own, with no human in the loop. It runs two agents side by side: a long-only Spot agent and a long/short Futures agent, both driven by the same macro trend and momentum logic.
